About Us


Late Adv. Dadasaheb Chavan Memorial Institute of Pharmacy was established by Shri Dhanalakshmi Foundation, Karad in 2006 for the development of specific qualities required for pharmacists. The institute offers diploma and bachelor’s degrees in pharmacy so as to promote the training and excellence of students to meet the global needs of the society through highly experienced and technical professors drawn from the vast pool of the pharmacy sector. Our campus ‘Adv. Dadasaheb Chavan Vidyasankul’ is located in a green and pollution free area at Malwadi (Masur), just 14 km from Karad city. The campus is surrounded by hills and forests which are managed by the District Forest Department in collaboration with us. It is useful to provide a sense of green environment and environmental friendliness to the members of the organization.

A bachelor’s degree in pharmacy offered by the institute is affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU), Lonere, Maharashtra, India. Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), New Delhi and Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra recognizes it. Diploma in Pharmacy course is affiliated with Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai. Most professors excel in the respective domains and are well known in the field of pharmacy for their postgraduate and doctoral degrees. With their commitment to bringing students to their academic place and improving employability, professors are relentlessly dedicating themselves to achieving the vision of the institution. In the process of accomplishing the motto of ‘achieving excellence’, the teachers are trying to develop scientific temper among students by means of distinguishing, analyzing and solving various pharmaceutical problems.

Our Vission and Mission

MISSION

In accordance with the above mentioned vision, the institute will strive to persue the following: M 1. Establish state of the art infrastructure which will contribute to capacity building of students. M 2. Develop learner centered pedagogical processes. M 3. Provide equality of educational opportunity & imbibe professional ethics among students to make them responsive to the needs of the society.

VISION

The institute exists to impart inclusive learning to professional aspirants & to nurture them to become effective and socially responsible individuals with the ultimate aim of increasing respect for the profession in society.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs)

PEO 1


Provide socially responsible, environment friendly solutions to pharmacy related broad-based problems adapting professional ethics.

PEO 2


Adapt state-of-the-art pharmaceutical broad-based technologies to work in multi-disciplinary work environments.

PEO 3


Solve broad-based problems individually and as a team member communicating effectively in the world of work
